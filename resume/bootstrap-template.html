About Me

Hello, I am Varsha Kori! My passion for the field of User Experience led me to pursue a graduate study in the same from the University of Maryland, College Park. I am keen on simplifying experiences for the user and designing accessible platforms for everyone. I believe that a good user experience is the one which does not make a user think. I lay emphasis on brainstorming and understanding the user needs during my design process. I want to build products that users want to pay for, share, and come back for, that is, Design Success = User Success = Business Success.

I love to travel, click pictures, and collect memories. Please feel free to go through my website and browse my background.
